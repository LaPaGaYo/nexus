Date: 2026-04-10

## Summary

This release turns the maintainer automation loop into the next published Nexus baseline.

## What Ships

- `nexus-maintainer-check` as the Nexus-owned maintainer status entrypoint
- repo-visible maintainer reports under `upstream-notes/maintainer-status.json` and `upstream-notes/maintainer-status.md`
- refresh contract synchronization for imported upstream snapshots and Nexus provenance anchors
- idempotent maintainer-status writes so release preflight is not blocked by timestamp churn
- contract-derived upstream refresh assertions to keep CI stable across future refreshes
